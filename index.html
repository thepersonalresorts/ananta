Yields, occupancy, and the unit economics

Net yields of 9–13% per annum are modeled at 70% year-round occupancy, with operator fee, OPEX, replacement reserve, and tax already deducted. Distributions are quarterly. We publish a full pro forma per unit on request — written, not whispered.

How a purchase actually works

Four steps: reservation deposit → SPA → milestone payments through construction → handover. Foreign ownership is structured via PT PMA (Canggu, branded residences) or 80-year leasehold with extension (Villas Bukit). Notary, escrow, and tax are arranged by us, paid by you.

Who runs it after you own it

Ananta is operated end-to-end by our in-house team — same brand, same standards across all properties. You can stay any week of the year (subject to booking); the rest of the calendar runs as short-stay rental. One contract, one invoice, one number to call.
