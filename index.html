A second home that pays rent — and holds value.

Ananta is built for ownership that earns. Operator-run end to end, with year-round occupancy and a single party accountable for both the asset and the income.

Yields, occupancy and unit economics

Net yields of 9–13% p.a. are modeled at 70% year-round occupancy, with operator fee, OPEX, replacement reserve and tax already deducted. Distributions quarterly. Full pro forma per unit on request.

How a purchase actually works

Reservation → SPA → milestone payments through construction → handover. Foreign ownership via PT PMA (Canggu, branded residences) or 80-year leasehold with extension (Villas Bukit). Notary, escrow, tax arranged by us, paid by you.

Who runs it after you own it

Ananta is operated end-to-end by our in-house team — same brand, same standards. You stay any week of the year (subject to booking); the rest runs as short-stay rental. One contract, one invoice, one number.

Three vows

Three vows — to the place, the owner, the guest.

I · To the place

Originality from the genius of place.

Ananta lives where it was born — the cliffs of Bukit, the rice fields of Canggu. We work with architects who design for the place, not against it: open air, monsoon-ready, walkable from beach to villa.

II · To the owner

Long-term durability and yield.

Operator-run end to end. Same team designs the unit, signs the deed, and runs the booking — net yield 9–13% p.a. on a 70% year-round occupancy model. One contract, one number to call.

III · To the guest

Something extra in every stay.

Resorts that offer guests something more — a story, a ritual, a quiet corner — that's worth coming back for. The kind of detail that turns a stay into a memory.
