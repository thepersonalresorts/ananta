Inside the villa

Detail by detail

The eight villas are built around four design moves - engineered for daily living, not for the brochure.

01

Private pool - the daily appointment

Every villa has its own pool. Glass edge facing the garden, layered planting for shade, an outdoor table that opens to the night sky.

02

Bedroom with floor-to-ceiling glass

Every bedroom has its own bathroom. The master suite opens to a full-height glass wall framing the tropical landscape - free-standing tub, generous walk-in shower.

03

Stone-island kitchen + open living

Massive stone island, full kitchen, large dining table - built for family meals or evening with friends. Naturally flows into the terrace and pool.

04

Vertical light & sky lights

Open galleries and roof lights connect the two floors vertically. Daylight reaches the full height of the interior - a play of light and shadow throughout the day.
